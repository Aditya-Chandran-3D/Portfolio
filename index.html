About Me

Hi, I'm Aditya Chandran, an animation generalist with expertise in 2D/3D character design, frame-by-frame animation, rigging, modeling, and texturing.

I'm always eager to explore innovative techniques, refine my craft, and bring fresh perspectives to my work. In my free time, I stay inspired by experimenting with new animation styles, learning emerging tools, and drawing inspiration from diverse art forms.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I hope you enjoy my work.
